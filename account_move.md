## 1. What Is `account_move`?

`account_move` is Odoo's **central accounting ledger header table**.  
Every financial event in the system — whether triggered by a human or by automation — produces exactly one `account_move` row (the "header") plus one or more `account_move_line` rows (the debit/credit splits).  

It holds **all of** the following in one table, distinguished by the `move_type` column:

| `move_type`   | Business Meaning                          | Count (total DB) |
|---------------|-------------------------------------------|-----------------|
| `entry`       | Internal journal entry (misc / auto)      | **26,764**       |
| `out_invoice` | Customer invoice (sales)                  | **4,060**        |
| `in_invoice`  | Vendor bill (purchase / payroll)          | **2,672**        |
| `in_refund`   | Credit note received from vendor          | 11               |
| `out_refund`  | Credit note issued to customer            | 6                |
| **Total**     |                                           | **33,513**       |

## 4. Why These Appear in an Operations ERP

This is an ISP field-operations ERP (EarthLink / Light Speed Iraq). The `account_move` table is **large and diverse** because:

1. **Inventory-heavy operations** — every cable, splice, tool, and consumable passing through the warehouse creates an automatic costing entry. With 22K+ inventory valuation entries, field operations directly drives accounting volume.

2. **Distributed cash management** — dozens of field technicians carry petty cash (IQD 100K–500K each per day). Each cash disbursement and collection is a journal entry in a named personal treasury journal.

3. **Payroll via vendor bills** — monthly salaries are processed as Vendor Bills (partner: `Payroll`, `Hulum Current`) rather than through a separate payroll module. This keeps salary costs flowing through `account_move_line` expense accounts.

4. **Approval-to-bill workflow** — cash-carrier and contractor approvals (`approval_request`) are approved inside the ERP and trigger Vendor Bills automatically, creating journal entries with the `approval_request_id` FK.

5. **Multi-entity structure** — EarthLink and Hulum are separate legal entities sharing the same Odoo instance. Intercompany transfers (`تعزيز رصيد`, `Seperate Supportive and LS`) appear as manual Miscellaneous journal entries.

6. **Asset depreciation** — NES TELECOM equipment (IQD 3B+), Odoo software licenses, and SLA contracts are amortized monthly via Odoo's asset module, generating scheduled draft entries already queued to 2039.

## 8. How This Serves Your Data Marts

`account_move` + `account_move_line` is the **financial backbone** of every mart you will build. The table is already clean enough for direct staging — 32,540 posted rows, structured grain, consistent FKs. Below is the full mart map.

---

### 8.1 `fct_journal_entries` — Core Financial Fact

**Grain:** one row per `account_move_line` (debit/credit split), filtered to `state = 'posted'`.

```
account_move_line (grain row)
  └── account_move          → date, move_type, state, ref, journal_id, partner_id, approval_request_id
  └── account_account       → code, name, account_type (income / expense / asset / liability)
  └── account_journal       → journal name, type (general / sale / purchase / cash)
  └── res_partner           → partner name, type
  └── res_currency          → currency
  └── res_users (create_uid)→ who posted it
```

**Measures available directly:**
- `debit`, `credit`, `balance` (debit − credit)
- `amount_currency` (foreign currency amount)
- `date` (posting date), `create_date` (entry date)

**Dimensions to enrich from other marts:**
- `approval_request_id` → links to approval mart (cash-carrier approvals)
- `stock_move_id` (on `account_move`) → links to inventory mart
- `partner_id` → links to partner/customer dimension

### 8.5 `fct_cash_treasury` — Cash Operations Mart

**Source:** `account_move` where journal `type = 'cash'` and `state = 'posted'`.

**Grain:** one row per cash journal entry (daily petty cash disbursement or collection).

Joins to:
- `account_journal` → treasury holder name (the field employee)
- `account_move_line` → which cash account was debited/credited
- `res_users (create_uid)` → who logged the entry

This mart powers field cash reconciliation and exposure reporting per technician.

---

### 8.6 `fct_inventory_valuation` — Stock Cost Mart

**Source:** `account_move` where journal = `Inventory Valuation` (type `general`), joined to `stock_valuation_layer` via `account_move.stock_move_id → stock_move.id`.

**Grain:** one row per inventory valuation event (stock move + cost).

This mart gives you:
- Cost of goods used per maintenance request
- AVCO unit cost evolution per product over time
- Variance between procurement price and consumption cost
